calcSunDamageScalar
====================================================================================================

calcSunDamageScalar is used when calculating the amount of damage to apply for the Sun Damage magic effect.

Event Data
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

damage
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

`number`_. The previously calculated sun damage.
